About us

There was a day; There was a time when we all met on the app. The primary purpose was to study a language. It was easy to send text messages but not so easy to record our voices. Sometimes it took more than 2 hours to record one sentence. But we did it and shared our experiences with others efficiently on how to study a language, and we thank everyone who participated. This was a memorable time for us as we progressed the most while creating our podcast that we hope you’ll enjoy - Raivis and Hisae.
